Location

Each year, the convention takes place at the Salle Jean Nohain on the route de Béthune located next center of the city and the Stadium Bollaert of Lens and very close to several public transport, ideal for those who wish to come without car. The cenvention is at a 15 minutes-walk from the train station of Lens and thanks to the Tadao network, it is easy to get there in public transport.

Dates

This very first edition of Lens manga will take place on the weekend of 16 to 17 December 2023, the convention will be open from Saturday from 10am to 7pm, as well as from Sunday from 10am to 5:30pm.

Guests

This year we have planned a multitude of guests from different horizons, with gaming, dubbing, French authors or of pop culture universe! With as guest of mark Bob Lennon or Brigitte Lecordier! But also a mystery guest who will be unveiled a week before the convention, find the list of all the guests in the guests tab.

Activities

During your visit, you will have access to more than 50 stands distributed in the room and on its exteriors to entertain you, but also allow you to buy a multitude of goodies or snacks straight comming from Japan! We offer also different activities on stage in order to spend a pleasant moment, you can find conferences of our guests, a competition and cosplay parade, karaoke as well as quizzes on different themes!

Partners

We have the honor to receive as a major partner la librairie des Otakus which will also be present as a stand on the convention (Warning French Manga only)! But also Bob Lennon with his Comic Strip Fortress of the Black Cauldron, available for sale on its stand!
